Perfect Pancakes

Description

Prep: 5mins Cook: 25mins Serves: 8 approx

Delicious and easy pancake batter recipe for every one in the family to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegatable oil, plus extra for frying
  • pinch of salt

Method

  1. Put the Flour, milk, eggs and salt into a large mixing bowl
  2. Whisk until you have a smooth thick paste. Add a little more milk if the mix is too thick.
  3. Heat the pan over a moderate heat, and just before cooking wipe it with an oiled kitchen paper
  4. Ladle some of the batter into the pan, making sure to spread the batter thin and evenly across the pan.
  5. Leave the pancake to cook for 30 seconds before flipping to cook the other side for the same.
  6. Once cooked on both sides ease a palette knife under the pancake and quickly lift onto a warm plate.
  7. Continue to cook the rest of the batter in the same way.
  8. Decorate and add toppings to the pancake as you like.
  9. Enjoy!
